Our Story

The Beginning
of Alvora

Alvora was born of the simple belief that jewellery can be as honest and pure as it is beautiful.

Jewellery for generations has the spirit of celebration, love stories and immortal memories. But purchasing it can often be confusing and unclear. We started Alvora to solve this experience.

Our aim is to integrate extraordinary craftsmanship, approved quality and absolute transparency so that each customer can confidently buy jewellery. Each piece made by Alvora is intentional, hand-crafted and ethically sourced. Everything — from our BIS hallmarked gold to certified diamonds and gemstones — reflects our commitment towards authenticity and trust.

Alvora is not just about jewellery — it is about making pieces that become part of your history and what you live for the next years or decades.
